About Kangaroos

Kangaroos are marsupials indigenous to Australia, known for their powerful legs, large tails, and pouches for carrying their young.

Kangaroo Species

Red Kangaroo

The largest of all kangaroo species, known for its reddish-brown fur.

Eastern Grey Kangaroo

Found in eastern Australia, this species has a soft grey coat.

Western Grey Kangaroo

Native to the southern part of Australia, distinguished by its shaggy coat.
