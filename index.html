About Xiaomi's Privacy Protection Policy

1. Introduction

At Xiaomi, they prioritize the privacy and security of users' personal information. This page Outlines how they collect, use, disclose and protect your data when you use Xiaomi products and services or visit their websites.

2. The information they collect

a. Information You Provide

They collect personal data that you provide to them when registering for a Xiaomi account, purchasing a product, or requesting support services, such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment information.

b. Automatically Collected Information

When you interact with their products or services, they automatically collect certain data, such as device identifiers, IP addresses, browsing behavior, and location information. This helps them improve the user experience and provide personalized service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • To provide and improve their products and services;
  • Process your orders and payments;
  • Communicate with you regarding your account, updates, and promotions;
  • Personalize your experience based on your preferences;
  • Ensure security and prevent fraudulent activities.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

Xiaomi does not sell your personal data. However, they may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted partners who provide services on their behalf, such as payment processors and logistics companies;
  • When required by law or government requests;
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Xiaomi, our users, or the public.

5. How them Protect Your Data

The security of your data is Paramount to them. Xiaomi has taken various technical and organizational measures, including:

  • Encryption of data during transmission and storage;
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments;
  • Access control measures to limit access to personal information to authorized personnel only.

6. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Correction: You may access and update your personal data at any time through your Xiaomi account.
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal information when it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience on our website, analyze trends, and administer the website. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
